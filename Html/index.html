Mision Our vision is to produce highly competent Engineers and quality Technocrats through continual improvement of the standards of excellence in teaching, training and research. Mission The mission of the institution is to provide practical training, improve analytical power, reasoning abilities and technical dexterity. The institution is committed to uphold standards of academic distinction. Since 1985 It is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and Recognized by UGC under 2F. The College is an ISO 9001:2008 certified institution. It offers 10 UG Courses, 9 PG Courses, MBA, MCA and Full time Ph.D., Mechanical Engineering and Information & Communication Engineering. Its main objective is to provide technical education of high standard to the young aspirants in the remote areas of Tamilnadu to pursue engineering education. RVS Educational Trust has widened its horizon from Technical Education to all popular disciplines including Medicine and Agriculture with more than 110 institutions under its fold. The Trust has provided infrastructure of very high quality facilitating the students to comprehend engineering subjects and acquire the required skills for their career.
