Master Of Computer Applications (MCA) MCA @ KPGCAS Our MCA Program at the KPGCAS College of Arts and Science, Sulur, has been offering students the best quality & updated technical skills years since its inception in 1989. The uniqueness of our MCA Program is that it's being offered from an autonomous college transforming the undergraduates into professionals through a digitally-enabled curriculum.

Eligibility CRITERIA

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA Passed BCA/Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science Engineering or equivalent degree, or B.Sc. /B.Com./B.A. With Mathematics at 10+2 level or Graduation Level. The student needs to obtain at least 50% marks (45% marks in the case of candidates belonging to the reserved category) in the Qualifying Examination.

ABOUT THE PROGRAMME

The two-year RVS CAS MCA Program is a full-time course that leads to 'Full Stack App Development' with a strong foundation of Web Development skills and Project Development skills to stay ahead.
